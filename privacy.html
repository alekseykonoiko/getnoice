Privacy Policy for Noice

Effective Date: December 24, 2024

1. Introduction

Noice ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application or website.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address, authentication tokens, and related identifiers required to create and manage your account.
  • Content Data: Audio recordings, transcriptions, user-generated notes, and any text or links you submit for processing (e.g., URLs).
  • Usage Data: Device type, operating system, app usage behavior, error logs, and interaction metrics.
  • Subscription and Billing Data: If you subscribe to a paid plan, we may collect information necessary to process payments via a secure third-party payment processor.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide core app functionality such as transcription, summarization, and note management.
  • Operate and maintain secure cloud services.
  • Improve service quality, performance, and user experience.
  • Track usage for subscription enforcement and quota management.
  • Communicate with you about updates, support, and optional marketing (if opted in).

4. Data Processing

Transcription and language processing tasks are performed on secure remote servers using advanced machine learning models. Your data is temporarily stored during processing and then associated with your account for later access.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with advertisers or third-party marketing platforms. We may share limited information with:

  • Service providers that help us operate the platform under strict confidentiality.
  • Legal authorities if required by law (e.g., court orders or valid government requests).

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit and access controls.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as necessary to provide the service and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting support.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data.
  • Request data export in a portable format.
  • Withdraw consent to marketing emails.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@getnoice.app.

9. Children's Privacy

Noice is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. If changes are material, we will notify you through the app or email. Continued use of the service means you accept the updated terms.
