Noice is a powerful mobile application that transforms conversations into actionable insights using AI-powered transcription technology.

  • 🎙️ Audio Recording: Crystal-clear audio recording for meetings and lectures
  • ✍️ Real-time Transcription: Instant speech-to-text conversion
  • 🤖 AI Summaries: Get smart summaries with key points and action items
  • 📱 Background Processing: Continue transcribing even when your screen is off
  • 🔔 Push Notifications: Get notified when your transcriptions are ready
  • 🔒 Secure Storage: Safe and accessible storage across all your devices
  • 🌙 Dark Mode: Comfortable viewing in any lighting condition
  • 🔐 Authentication: Secure sign-in with email, Google, and Apple accounts
